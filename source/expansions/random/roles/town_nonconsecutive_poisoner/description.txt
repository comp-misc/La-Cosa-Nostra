Role Description:

"Welcome to _____! You are a Town Nonconsecutive Poisoner.

Role Abilities:
- You may each night choose to poison a player. Unless they are cured either this or the next night, they will die. They will be informed of this.
Your action may not be used on two nights consecutively.

Win Condition: You win when all threats to town has been eliminated and there is at least one member of town left."
