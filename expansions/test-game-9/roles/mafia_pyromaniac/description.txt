Fiery-tempered, compounded with a particular obsession with fire. That's you.

Every night, you may vote to douse or ignite a player. If there is a Pyrofather, you will douse or ignite at their command. If they are unavailable, you will douse or ignite on your own accord.

Doused players are not notified that they have been doused. Ignition deals an Unstoppable attack to all doused players.
