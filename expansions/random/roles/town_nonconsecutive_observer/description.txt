Role Description:

"Welcome to _____! You are a Town Nonconsecutive Observer.

Role Abilities:
- You may each night choose to simultaneously track and watch a player. You will recieve a report the following morning containing who the player visited and was visited by, however both reports are shuffled into one.
Your action may not be used on two nights consecutively.

Win Condition: You win when all threats to town has been eliminated and there is at least one member of town left."
