Role Description:

"Welcome to _____! You are a Mafia 1-shot Jailkeeper.

Role Abilities:
- You can at night, once during the game, choose to jail a player. As a result the player is unable to do any actions, in addition to being protected from a single night attack.

Factional Abilities:
- You are given a common mafia night chat with the other mafias.
- You may each night use the mafia factional kill on a player.

Win Condition: You win when mafia gains majority and all other threats are eliminated."
